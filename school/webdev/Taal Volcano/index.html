About Taal Volcano

Taal Volcano, located in the province of Batangas, is one of the Philippines' most active volcanoes. It is famous for being an island within a lake, which is itself on an island within a larger lake.

Its distinctive geological setting has attracted tourists, scientists, and nature enthusiasts worldwide.
