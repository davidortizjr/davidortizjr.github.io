Taal Volcano Facts

Interesting Facts

  • Taal Volcano is the second most active volcano in the Philippines.
  • The volcano is part of a complex volcano system with 47 craters and cones.
  • It is located on the island of Luzon and is part of the Pacific Ring of Fire.
  • The last major eruption occurred in January 2020, causing significant ashfall.
  • Taal Volcano sits in the middle of Taal Lake, a large freshwater lake in Batangas.
